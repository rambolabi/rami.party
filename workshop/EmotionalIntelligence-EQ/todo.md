# Emotional Intelligence (EQ) — Workshop Project

## Overview
Interactive workshop on emotional intelligence (Goleman's four domains).
Part of **The People Library** (../personality-library/index.html).

## Delivered
- Shared engine (index.html / style.css / app.js), green accent.
- `data.js` — content, `score` assessment (15 statements → banded EQ reading with advice + meter).
- Concept cards (self-awareness, self-management, empathy, social skill), grow-it guide, FAQ.
- Responsive, accessible, print-friendly. No backend.

## Notes
- Emphasis: EQ is learnable, unlike IQ.
- Self-report is a mirror; recommend pairing with honest feedback from others.
