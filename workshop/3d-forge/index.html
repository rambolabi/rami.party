    3D Forge — quick help

    Everything runs offline. Nothing you add ever leaves your browser.

    • Add objects from the left toolbar: text, emoji, shapes, QR, or a photo.
    • Lithophane: upload a portrait — dark areas print thick (block light), light areas thin. Toggle 💡 Backlight to preview.
    • Move / rotate / scale the selected object from the Properties panel — all in real millimetres.
    • Watertight badge confirms the model will slice without repair warnings.
    • Export and pick your slicer — the right format & settings are chosen for you. 3MF is best for Bambu/Prusa/Orca/Cura.
