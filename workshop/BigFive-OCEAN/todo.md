# The Big Five (OCEAN) — Workshop Project

## Overview
Interactive workshop on the Big Five, the most research-backed model of personality.
Part of **The People Library** (../personality-library/index.html).

## Delivered
- Shared data-driven engine (index.html / style.css / app.js), blue accent.
- `data.js` — Big Five content, `profile` assessment (15 items → 5 trait meters with interpretations).
- Concept cards, handle/apply guide, FAQ, DISC colour cross-link.
- Responsive, accessible, print-friendly. No backend.

## Notes
- Assessment mode `profile`: per-trait Likert scoring with reverse items.
- Framed responsibly: spectrums not types; higher isn't "better"; short self-report, not clinical.
