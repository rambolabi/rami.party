# Conflict Styles (Thomas–Kilmann) — Workshop Project

## Overview
Interactive workshop on the five conflict-handling modes (Thomas–Kilmann).
Part of **The People Library** (../personality-library/index.html).

## Delivered
- Shared engine (index.html / style.css / app.js), orange accent.
- `data.js` — content, `classify` assessment (10 questions → one of five styles), 5 profiles.
- Concept cards (two dimensions, no best mode, flexing), apply guide, FAQ.
- Responsive, accessible, print-friendly. No backend.

## Notes
- Styles: competing, collaborating, compromising, avoiding, accommodating.
- Emphasis on situational flexing rather than a single "best" style.
