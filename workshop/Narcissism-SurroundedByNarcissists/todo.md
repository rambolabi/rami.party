# Surrounded by Narcissists — Web Application Project

## Project Overview
An educational, interactive workshop based on Thomas Erikson's *Surrounded by Narcissists*.
It teaches the four faces of narcissism and how to hold your ground — framed as behavioural
awareness, **not** clinical diagnosis.

## Key Requirements
- Educational content: the spectrum, the four faces, the idealise/devalue/discard cycle,
  narcissistic supply & the false self, holding your ground.
- Observer assessment (classify): 12 observations → which of four styles (Grandiose,
  Vulnerable, Communal, Malignant) fits best, with a distribution bar chart.
- Handle / field guide: Do / Don't + grey-rock & boundaries.
- FAQ with responsible disclaimers.

## Design & UX
- Clean, modern, professional, purple accent.
- Responsive, accessible, print-friendly, one question at a time, result at end.

## Technical Implementation
- Shared data-driven engine (`app.js`) + `style.css`.
- Book-specific content in `data.js` (`BOOK` object, assessment mode `classify`).
- No backend, static site.

## Content Authority & Responsibility
Inspired by *Surrounded by Narcissists* by Thomas Erikson. Educational only — not diagnostic.
Footer invites readers to buy the book and support the author.
