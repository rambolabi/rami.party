The Erikson workshops are inspired by Surrounded by Idiots, Psychopaths, Narcissists, Bad Bosses, Liars, Vampires and Setbacks by Thomas Erikson — if they resonate, read the books and support the author. The other workshops draw on established models (DISC, the Big Five, MBTI, the Enneagram, attachment theory, the love languages, Thomas–Kilmann and emotional intelligence). All are educational tools for reflection, not clinical diagnosis.
