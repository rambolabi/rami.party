# The Five Love Languages — Workshop Project

## Overview
Interactive workshop on Gary Chapman's five love languages.
Part of **The People Library** (../personality-library/index.html).

## Delivered
- Shared engine (index.html / style.css / app.js), rose accent.
- `data.js` — content, `classify` assessment (10 forced-choice pairs → primary language), 5 profiles.
- Concept cards, apply guide, FAQ.
- Responsive, accessible, print-friendly. No backend.

## Notes
- Forced-choice pairs cover all 10 language combinations for balance.
- Footer credits Gary Chapman and invites readers to buy the book.
