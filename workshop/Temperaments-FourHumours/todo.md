# The Four Temperaments — Workshop Project

## Overview
Interactive workshop on the classical four temperaments (Hippocrates / Galen) and how they map
onto DISC and modern models. Part of **The People Library** (../personality-library/index.html).

## Delivered
- Shared engine (index.html / style.css / app.js), violet accent.
- `data.js` — content, `classify` assessment (10 questions → one of four temperaments), 4 profiles.
- Concept cards (origins, modern map, blends), apply guide, FAQ, DISC colour cross-link (direct map).
- Responsive, accessible, print-friendly. No backend.

## Notes
- Temperaments: sanguine, choleric, melancholic, phlegmatic → DISC yellow/red/blue/green.
- Notes clearly that the "humours" biology is discredited; the behavioural patterns endure.
