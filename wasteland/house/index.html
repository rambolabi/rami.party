Help & Information

How to Use This House Inspection Tool

1. Enter Property Information

Fill in the property details at the top: address, contact person, date, and time. This information will appear in your final report.

2. Use Category Filters

Click category buttons to focus on specific areas (Kitchen, Bathroom, etc.) or use renovation filters to see potential renovation items.

3. Dual Checkbox System

  • ✓ OK Checkbox (Green): Mark items that pass inspection
  • ⚠ Issue Checkbox (Orange): Mark items needing attention or renovation
  • Request Button (Documents): Track which documents need to be requested

4. Add Notes

Click on any item to expand notes. Add specific observations, measurements, or concerns for each item.

5. Generate Report

Click "Generate Report" to create a comprehensive inspection summary. The report includes property info, OK items, issues found, documents to request, and all your notes.

Auto-Save Feature

All your data is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. You can close the page and return later - your progress will be preserved.

GDPR Compliance

Data Storage

This application stores all data locally in your browser using HTML5 localStorage. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

Your Rights

  • Right to Access: All your data is visible on this page
  • Right to Deletion: Use the "Reset All" button to delete all stored data
  • Right to Portability: Use "Copy to Clipboard" to export your data
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit any information at any time

Data Controller

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are the data controller. Compyra does not collect, process, or have access to any of your inspection data.

Cookies

This application does not use cookies. It only uses localStorage for saving your inspection progress on your device.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. This is a client-side application that runs entirely in your browser. All information you enter stays on your device.

Local Storage Only

The application uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Property information you enter
  • Checkbox states (OK/Issue)
  • Notes and observations
  • Theme preferences (dark/light mode)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone else.

No Server Communication

This application does not:

  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Connect to databases
  • Share information with third parties

Data Deletion

To delete all stored data:

  • Click the "Reset All" button in the header
  • Clear your browser's localStorage
  • Clear your browser's cache and site data

Device-Specific Storage

Data is stored per device and per browser. If you use a different device or browser, you will start with a clean slate.
